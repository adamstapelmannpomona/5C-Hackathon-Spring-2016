Our Story

After their first year at Pomona College Adam, Errol and Juan knew there had to be more to life than just the “Claremont Bubble.” The trio spent the majority of their first year looking for ways to get off campus and explore the surrounding areas, but travel restrictions, expensive prices, and strict time limits hindered their ambitions. Then it dawned on them – create a carpool system that allowed students to travel seamlessly from their campus to various destinations, to make new friends, and to make the most of their free time.

What started as an idea to help college students get off campus and escape “the bubble” has now altered the logistical fabric of travel within Southern California. Whether it’s a ride to the grocery store, to the beach or to the airport, HOVr allows people to go where they want when they want and to make some new friends along the way.
